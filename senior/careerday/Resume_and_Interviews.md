Interviewing
===

Preparing tips
***

* Dress appropriatly
* Mock interviews
* Research company
* Relevant questions

During the interview
***

* Be early, not too early
* Hard copy of resume
* Eye contact
* Clarify questions and take time if needed
* Focus on yourself, not the team
* Stay objective
